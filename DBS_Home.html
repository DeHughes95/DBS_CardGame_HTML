About

Dragon Ball Super is a card game in which you create a 50-60 card deck in addition to a Leader Card which will start in play. The goal is to take your oppenent to 0 life from 8 which will be determined by face down cards put into play during setup. (There are alternative win conditions but we will mention those later) Game starts with 6 cards in hand player who goes first may not attack on turn 1.
